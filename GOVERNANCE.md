\# PRAXIS TITAN — Governance \& License Intent



\## 1. Governance Model (Design Intent)



PRAXIS TITAN is designed as a \*\*governed analytic engine\*\*:

\- Offline execution

\- Human-initiated runs

\- Artifact-driven traceability

\- No autonomous actuation



The system is intended for research, engineering analysis, and decision support.



---



\## 2. Non-Goals / Prohibited Uses (Safety Boundary)



PRAXIS TITAN is not intended for:

\- Autonomous control of real-world systems

\- Safety-critical operational deployment without formal certification

\- Real-time operational decision automation

\- Financial trading automation

\- Any use requiring unsupervised actuation
