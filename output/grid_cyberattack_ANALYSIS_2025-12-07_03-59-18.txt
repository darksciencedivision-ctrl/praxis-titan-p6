3) RESEARCH QUESTIONS
=====================================================

1. How can transformer efficiency be improved to reduce the risk of overload?
2. Can machine learning algorithms predict control system malfunctions before they occur?
3. What are the most effective strategies for mitigating the risks associated with cyber-physical attacks on critical infrastructure?
4. How can grid stability be enhanced during extreme weather events, such as winter storms or heat waves?
5. Is it possible to develop more accurate models of cascading failures in power grids to improve preventive measures?
6. Can AI-based control systems better anticipate and respond to load fluctuations than traditional methods?
7. What are the most effective techniques for detecting and responding to malware infections in grid control systems?
8. How can we reduce the likelihood of equipment failure causing widespread blackouts?
9. Are there ways to enhance the resilience of power grids against coordinated attacks on multiple components?
10. Can advanced fault prediction models help prevent catastrophic failures, such as large-scale blackouts?
